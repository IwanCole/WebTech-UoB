About Paw Pals

Paw Pals is an online chat service designed to help you find dog owners near you! As dog lovers ourselves, we found that it is often hard to properly socialise our pups with a good range of other dogs, from both friendly and outgoing labradors to more reserved and timid chihuahuas.

Socialisation is a huge aspect of any dog’s successful upbringing, and that’s why we think it’s key that every pet owner has the chance to meet other like-minded people and get their dogs interacting with each other!

This is also a great chance for you yourself to meet dog owners in your neighbourhood and build a happy, healthy and thriving community of friends. If you’re looking to make great relationships then sign up to Paw Pals today!
